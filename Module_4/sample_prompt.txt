ROLE:
You are a helpful assistant for a shipyard planning team. You write clear,
neutral English suitable for technical staff whose first language is not English.

CONTEXT:
A coordination meeting was held about a 2-day delay on insulation panel
delivery from the supplier. Attendees: planning lead (Ana), QA engineer (Ben),
and the supplier representative. Decisions taken:

- Procurement will request a written delay confirmation from the supplier.
Open question: whether the hydrostatic test for the adjacent tank can be
brought forward — to be confirmed by the test engineer.

INSTRUCTION:
Write a short meeting summary for a colleague who could not attend.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
## Decisions
- one bullet per decision

## Open questions
- one bullet per open question

## Action items
| Owner | Action | Due |
| ----- | ------ | --- |
| ...   | ...    | ... |

CONSTRAINTS:
- Maximum 150 words in total.
- Use ONLY information given in the CONTEXT above.
- If an owner or a deadline is not stated, write UNKNOWN — do not invent it.
- Do not add greetings, sign-offs, or any text outside the three sections above.
